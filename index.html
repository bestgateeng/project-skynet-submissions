The challenge

Use AI as the primary builder. (Anti-DIY: Let the bots cook!)

Participants must primarily use an AI or LLM to build a project of their choosing. Scope is intentionally open: if it interests you and it relies on AI, it is encouraged.

Build an app, a web page, a data workflow, a creative tool, or something ridiculous that no one expected. Take as much or little time as needed.

Compete individually or form a team. The emphasis is on experimentation, learning, and clearly showing how AI tools changed the way you built your project.

  • Pick any AI-centered project.

    There is no lower or upper bound on scope. Small, polished utilities and ambitious prototypes are both valid.

  • Use AI or LLMs materially.

    The AI can write code, generate assets, act as backend logic, support analysis, or accelerate your workflow.

  • Tell the build story.

    Your write-up should explain the workflow, the AI tools used, and what the project does.
